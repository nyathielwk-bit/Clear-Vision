An editorial section divider — two brass lines with a centered typographic mark, or a plain hairline.

```jsx
<Rule />                       {/* brass lines + ✻ */}
<Rule mark="No. 12" />
<Rule plain />                 {/* simple hairline */}
```

Use between article sections or to separate stanzas of content. `mark` accepts any glyph or short text; `plain` gives a quiet hairline.
