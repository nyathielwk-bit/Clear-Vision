A large italic-Playfair pull quote with a brass rule and attribution — the signature editorial flourish for articles and features.

```jsx
<Pullquote cite="Mara Devlin" role="Founding Editor" align="center">
  We went looking for the perfect everyday bowl and found a philosophy instead.
</Pullquote>
```

`align`: left / center. `cite` + `role` render the attribution under a brass rule; set `mark={false}` to hide the opening quote glyph.
